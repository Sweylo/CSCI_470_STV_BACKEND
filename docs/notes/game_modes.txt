game modes:

 - fast game (maxed pawns or one level bishop/rook/knight)

 - easy     - show threatened pieces and if it will be taken where you move
            - show potential moves
            - upgrade to defense path only
            - limited to 8x8 board

 - normal   - no training wheels
            - only upgrade attack path
            - small to medium board size (8x8 or larger)

 - expert   - all boards
            - upgrade to both paths

power cards (bring in up to 3 cards at beginning of match):

 - instant piece kill count increment (applies after the turn played)
